Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll Out the Dough:
    • Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Top the Pizza:
    • Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
    • Add your desired toppings on top of the cheese.
  4. Bake the Pizza:
    • Drizzle the olive oil over the assembled pizza.
    • Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper over the top.
    • Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Slice and Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
    • Slice the pizza into wedges or squares and serve hot.
    • Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!

Serving Tips:

For an extra burst of flavor, sprinkle some red pepper flakes or fresh basil leaves on top of the pizza before serving. Serve with a side of marinara sauce or ranch dressing for dipping.
