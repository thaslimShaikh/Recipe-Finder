Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 2 teaspoons biryani masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 4 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron strands soaked in 1/4 cup warm milk

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Rice:
    • Wash the basmati rice under cold running water until the water runs clear.
    • Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a bowl, mix the yogurt, ginger, garlic, green chilies, coriander leaves, mint leaves, biryani masala powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
    • Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and mix well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Prepare the Biryani:
    • In a large skillet or pot, heat the ghee or oil over medium heat.
    • Add the cumin seeds and sliced onions. Cook until the onions turn golden brown.
    • Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
    • Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is half-cooked.
    • Layer half of the soaked rice over the chicken, then sprinkle with saffron milk.
    • Layer the remaining rice over the saffron milk.
    • Cover the skillet or pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice and chicken are fully cooked.
    • Once cooked, gently fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.

Serving Tips:

Chicken Biryani pairs well with raita (yogurt sauce), sliced cucumbers, and a wedge of lemon. Garnish with fried onions and fresh cilantro leaves before serving for added flavor and aroma.
