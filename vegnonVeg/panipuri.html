Pani Puri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 20 puris (puffed semolina or wheat balls)
  • 1 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1 cup green chutney
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon black salt
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pani:
    • Mix tamarind chutney and green chutney with 4 cups of water in a bowl to make the pani.
    • Add chopped coriander leaves, mint leaves, roasted cumin powder, black salt, chaat masala, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
    • Chill the pani in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  2. Assemble the Pani Puri:
    • Make a small hole in the center of each puri using your thumb or a spoon.
    • Fill each puri with a spoonful of mashed potatoes and cooked chickpeas.
    • Top with chopped onions and a drizzle of tamarind chutney and green chutney.
    • Sprinkle some roasted cumin powder, black salt, and chaat masala on top.
    • Squeeze a few drops of lemon juice on each puri.

Serving Tips:

Arrange the assembled puris on a serving plate. Serve immediately with the chilled pani. Enjoy each crispy puri filled with tangy and spicy flavors!
