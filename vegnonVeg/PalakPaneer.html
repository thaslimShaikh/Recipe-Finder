Palak Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 10 oz (280g) paneer, cut into cubes
  • 16 oz (450g) fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 medium onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 inch (2.5 cm) ginger, roughly chopped
  • 8 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
  • 2 green chilies, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup (30g) cashew nuts
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black salt (kala namak)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp dried fenugreek leaves (kasoori methi), crushed
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) heavy whipping cream

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the spinach leaves in boiling water for 4 minutes, then transfer to ice-cold water to stop the cooking process. Drain and blend into a smooth puree.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the onion, ginger, garlic, green chilies, cashews, and 1/2 cup (120ml) water. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes or until the onion is soft and translucent. Blend into a smooth puree.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the tomato and cook until soft and mushy. Add the onion puree and simmer for 2-3 minutes. Add the spinach puree, salt, black salt, garam masala, and crushed kasoori methi. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the paneer and heavy cream. Simmer for 1-2 minutes, then remove from heat. Serve hot with naan or rice.
