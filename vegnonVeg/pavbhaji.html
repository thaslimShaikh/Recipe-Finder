Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (cauliflower, carrots, peas, capsicum), finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 pav (dinner rolls), lightly toasted with butter
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Bhaji:
    • Heat oil and butter in a pan.
    • Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
    • Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
    • Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
    • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
    • Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
    • Add mixed vegetables and mashed potatoes. Mix thoroughly.
    • Add a little water if the mixture is too dry. Mash the vegetables while cooking.
    • Cook for 10-15 minutes on medium heat until the vegetables are soft and well combined.
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  2. Prepare the Pav:
    • Slit the pav horizontally without cutting through.
    • Spread some butter on a griddle or tawa.
    • Place the pav on the griddle and toast until golden brown and crispy.

Serving Tips:

Serve hot Pav Bhaji garnished with fresh coriander leaves, lemon wedges, and a dollop of butter. Enjoy with toasted pav!
