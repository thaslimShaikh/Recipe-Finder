Chicken Roll Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 4 parathas or rotis
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a bowl, mix ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
    • Add chicken breasts to the marinade and coat them well. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
    • Heat oil in a pan and cook the marinated chicken breasts until cooked through. Let them cool, then shred the chicken.
  2. Assemble the Roll:
    • Warm the parathas or rotis.
    • Spread a tablespoon each of mayonnaise and ketchup on one side of the paratha or roti.
    • Place sliced onion, cucumber, tomato, and shredded chicken on top.
    • Sprinkle some fresh coriander leaves.
    • Roll the paratha or roti tightly and secure with a toothpick if necessary.

Serving Tips:

Serve Chicken Roll hot, garnished with additional sauce or chutney if desired. Enjoy as a delicious snack or light meal!
