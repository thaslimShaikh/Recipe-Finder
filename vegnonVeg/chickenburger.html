Chicken Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken thighs
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken:
    • Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
    • Grill or pan-fry the chicken until fully cooked and golden brown on both sides.
    • Place a slice of cheese on each chicken thigh and allow it to melt.
  2. Assemble the Burger:
    • Cut the burger buns in half and toast them lightly.
    • Spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun and mustard sauce on the top half.
    • Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each bun.
    • Top with a cooked chicken thigh, followed by sliced tomatoes and onions.
    • Drizzle ketchup over the toppings.
    • Cover with the top half of the bun.
