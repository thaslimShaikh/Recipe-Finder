Jalebi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder (optional, for color)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron strands (optional)
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Batter:
    • Mix all-purpose flour, gram flour, yogurt, baking soda, and turmeric powder in a bowl. Add water as needed to make a smooth batter.
    • Cover and let the batter ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight.
  2. Prepare the Syrup:
    • Heat water and sugar in a pan until the sugar dissolves completely.
    • Add saffron strands (if using) and simmer for 5 minutes to make a syrup of one-string consistency. Keep warm.
  3. Fry the Jalebis:
    • Heat oil or ghee in a wide, shallow pan.
    • Pour the batter into a squeeze bottle or piping bag with a small nozzle.
    • Squeeze the batter into hot oil in a circular motion to form spiral shapes.
    • Fry until the jalebis are crisp and golden brown.
    • Remove from oil and immediately dip them into warm sugar syrup.
    • Let them soak for a minute, then remove and serve hot.
