Loaded Nachos

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of tortilla chips (your favorite brand)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (divided)
  • 1 can (16 oz) of refried beans
  • 1 lb ground beef (or ground turkey, chicken, or beans for a vegetarian option)
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, sour cream, salsa, avocado, cilantro, etc.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  3. Add the diced onion, bell pepper, and jalapeño to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are soft.
  4. Add the garlic, chili powder, and cumin to the skillet and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Stir in the refried beans and cook until heated through.
  6. Arrange the tortilla chips in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
  7. Spoon the beef and bean mixture over the tortilla chips, leaving a small border around the edges.
  8. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of the shredded cheese over the top of the nachos.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and top with the remaining 1/2 cup of cheese, as well as any desired toppings (such as diced tomatoes, sour cream, etc.).
  11. Serve immediately and enjoy!
