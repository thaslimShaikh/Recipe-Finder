Spicy Ramen Scramble Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 package of instant ramen noodles (any flavor)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, sliced
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce (or more to taste)
  • Optional toppings: sliced green onions, chopped cilantro, shredded cheese, etc.

Instructions

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions, but do not add the seasoning packet.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion, bell pepper, jalapeño, and garlic, and cook until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add the cooked ramen noodles to the skillet, along with the soy sauce and sriracha sauce. Stir to combine and cook for 1-2 minutes, until the noodles are coated in the sauce.
  4. Push the noodle mixture to one side of the skillet. Crack the eggs into the other side of the skillet and scramble them until cooked through.
  5. Combine the eggs and noodles, and cook for 1 more minute, until everything is heated through.
  6. Serve the Spicy Ramen Scramble hot, with any desired toppings (such as sliced green onions, chopped cilantro, shredded cheese, etc.). Enjoy!
