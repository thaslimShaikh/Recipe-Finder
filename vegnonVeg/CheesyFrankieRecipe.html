Cheesy Frankie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the stuffing:
    • 2 tsp oil
    • ½ onion, finely chopped
    • ½ capsicum, finely chopped
    • 2 medium potato, boiled & mashed
    • 3 tbsp peri peri sauce / chilli sauce
    • salt to taste
  • Other ingredients:
    • 2 tsp butter
    • 3 chapati / wrap, leftover
    • 3 tsp mayonnaise, homemade / store-brought
    • 1 cup cabbage, thinly shredded
    • 6 tbsp cheddar / mozzarella cheese, grated

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the stuffing by heating oil in a pan and sautéing onion until translucent.
  2. Add capsicum and sauté for a minute or two.
  3. Add mashed potato and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add peri peri sauce and salt to taste, and mix well.
  5. Warm up leftover chapati or wrap with butter on a tawa.
  6. Spread mayonnaise on the chapati or wrap.
  7. Spread 2 tbsp of prepared stuffing on top of the mayonnaise.
  8. Top with shredded cabbage and grated cheese.
  9. Roll tight and wrap with aluminum foil for a street side feel.
  10. Serve Cheesy Frankie or pack to your lunch box.
